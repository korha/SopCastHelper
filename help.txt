Helper for SopCast app (www.sopcast.com)
Save PC resources for currently unwanted broadcasts without disconnecting.

Additional buttons:
"-" - Collapse application window for compact view;
"+" - Restore original size;
"M" - Mute process;
"U" - Unmute process;
Lock - Stub for an external player - load broadcast without playing;
Stream - Open broadcast in your external player. Path to player stored in SopCast.exe.ini (file must be in UTF-16LE without BOM).

Put all files to the application folder.
